Vic\u2019s Insights and Inspirations<\/h3>\n

We have entered a new era of ‘once upon a time’, where the reappearance of mythical dragons signal the emergence of a conscious global culture.\u00a0 … \u00a0Thus begins a new evolution of sacred technologies gifted to the world in the forms of inspiration, innovation, and invention – expressions of human-kind’s collective awakening of mind, body, and soul.<\/p>\n

Personal Vision<\/strong><\/span>
\nThe creation of sixty four globally-aligned technical incubation centers are used to realize new and advanced forms of innovation relating to advancing global mythologies that result in new Creation and the making of technologies that are Sacred and symbiotic within natural rhythms and planetary culture. This innovation matrix advances the design of communities using \u2018whole system\u2019 knowledge, realized within each center and shared world-wide, known as a ‘leadership ecology’. A community learning framework, along with unique mapping processes, are used to direct each center within the innovation matrix. Ecological design principles and sustainable business practices are followed that adapt to the needs of a globally conscious society. Collaboration methods are used to guide participants toward creating solutions that collectively serve and support a diverse yet interdependent, thriving world culture system.<\/p>\n

Message for the Future<\/strong><\/span>
\nThere are no rules, only choices. Let awe be your guide, let your walk be sacred, and learn to know who you are by being and watching that which you think you are not. For it is in becoming aware of the difference that arises an awakening – a place where we each find our Selves.<\/p>\n

Prophecy<\/strong><\/span>
\nThe World will come to know Itself as a conscious being, identified as Gaia, a sentient being that is born through its own collective insight. The time is near when we will gaze intently upon each other and feel the expression of Spirit impressed by the Knowing of each other\u2019s Soul.<\/p>\n

APPLICATION<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

A VISION FOR THE CREATION OF A LEARNING COMMUNITIES NETWORK USED TO TRANSITION A GLOBAL CULTURE IN CRISIS<\/strong><\/em><\/p>\n

Creating an Open Design Matrix for Realizing New Forms of Sustainable Wealth and Wellbeing<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

The creation of 64 globally-aligned incubation centers are used to realize new and advanced forms of design and innovation relating to organizational practices and technologies that are symbiotic with natural rhythms and planetary culture. This innovation matrix will help to advance the design of communities using \u2018whole system\u2019 knowledge that is realized within the centers and shared world-wide. Ecological design principles and sustainable business practices are followed that adapt to the needs of a globally conscious society. An eighteen community domain learning framework is used, along with a unique concentrix management process, to direct the centers within the innovation matrix. Collaborative learning methods are used to guide participants to create product and service solutions that support a diverse, interdependent, thriving world culture system.<\/p>\n

Building a Global Network of Associated Learning Communities<\/strong><\/span><\/p>\n

I would like to see a unique form of collaboration and design network occur across the world. Its creation will be comprised of a model for bringing people together with common passions and interests who want to participate in the changes at hand, both at the visionary and practical levels of implementation. The frameworks for gathering and connecting people will be very open, allow for group and individual empowerment, be guided by principles of sustainability and collaborative learning, and allow those with knowledge and wisdom to lead rather than those with (so-called) power strength and money, and (rather than our present unhealthy forms for discussing and debating) it will be based on an open dialogue system for sharing and learning from a world of people who are wealthy with ideas. Together, from community-to-community, we will create a wonderful new reality.<\/p>\n\n
