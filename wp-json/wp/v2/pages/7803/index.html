Sample Values Map Summary Chart<\/h1>\n

The following sample will help you to see how a values assessment is used to map organizational values. By combining yours with others a collective map can be determined. Most important is the process of awareness – how you and your co-hearts come to learn what is important to each other and how to support it. This is the beginning of a truly sustainable organization, which is why I am so enthusiastic about this work.<\/p>\n
