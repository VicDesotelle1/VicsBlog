Just as sustainability is defined in many different ways, these three E’s are also used in many different ways. ‘Ecology’ for example, is often used as another word for ‘environment’ when actually the word means interdependency of systems. For me, the word equity means the ability for a community to offer diverse choices to its stakeholders and community members and depends on those members having the know-how and ability to accept the responsibility of making healthier choices from a broader framework of understanding and questioning. Choices that can sustain not only the individuals doing the choosing but also enhance the well-being of the greater community.<\/span><\/span><\/p>\n
