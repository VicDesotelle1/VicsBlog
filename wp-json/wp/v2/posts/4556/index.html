Imagine a new way of thinking regarding what government is …\u00a0<\/strong><\/h4>\n

Rather than government being the provider of fixes, it instead becomes a convener of stakeholders, thereby allowing ‘we the people’ to generate our own solutions, which is how a real democracy should be. This\u00a0shifts our system from a STATIC<\/em> governMENT institution into a DYNAMIC<\/em> ‘governANCE’ system<\/span><\/strong>.<\/span> We go from rigid structure to flowing process. The governing body becomes a manager (or governor) of the ecology of interactions<\/em> that happen by the people. Collaboration then, becomes the vehicle that acts as the governor, as it enables the flow of action and change.<\/em> In this way, collaboration and governance become synonymous.\u00a0<\/span><\/strong><\/p>\n
