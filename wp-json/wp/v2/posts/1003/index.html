Regenerative Commerce<\/strong> is a system based on a broader understanding of what it means to develop technology and business. I propose that, using these principles, a region’s economic system can revitalize itself and perform beyond today’s assumptions and expectations. The following represent shifts in awareness of the models we use to create and innovate within enterprise. Note that a ‘shift’ means we are including new information that expands existing models and does not mean that we are replacing them.<\/p>\n

<\/a>DiscoveryFuel.com’s long term objective is to create a global network of innovation learning centers<\/strong>. Each interdependent center will incubate innovative technologies and companies that can work for a our emerging globally conscious community.<\/p>\n

This concept, known as ‘Regenerative Commerce<\/strong>‘, will act as an integrating framework for incorporating principles of sustainable innovation for the realization of business products and services that work for a post-industrial society.<\/p>\n
