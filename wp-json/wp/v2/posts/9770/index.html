My interpretation of people, planet, profit goes like this:\u00a0Elitist PEOPLE who PROFIT from others get to use the PLANET however they want, and make the laws that allow them to do so.<\/em><\/p>\n

Below is a great example of this, and how \u2018doing good\u2019 in order to \u2018save the planet\u2019 has nothing to do with human well being. Unless of course you are one of the monetary elites.\u00a0And so what about the rest of us??<\/p>\n

Look. If an organization like EPA is going to create over-arching rules that ban things, they have to also create solutions to replace what will be taken away by the ban.\u00a0In this case, it means giving every poor person, whose wood stove is no longer usable by law, a stove that is useable. This has to be added to the policy or guess what? It\u2019s not \u201csustainable\u201d. \u2026 Duh.<\/p>\n

Well, it\u2019s time for me to go find a cold rock to sleep under, because my wood stove has just now been banned. Sleep tight all you environmental extremists who have a pay check that allows you to buy your warmth, and ignore what is really happening here.<\/p>\n
